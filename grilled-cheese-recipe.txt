Ingredients:
 - Bread
 - Sliced cheddar cheese
 - Butter (1/4 Cup)


Directions:
 1. Place a non-stick skillet on the stovetop and set to low-medium heat.
 
 2. Once warm, place butter in the skillet.

 3. After butter melts, put first slice of bread into skillet, and build sandwhich. 
 
 4. Cook until cheese melted and bread is golden brown.
